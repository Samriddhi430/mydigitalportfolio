LWAED Model Details

LWAED is a lightweight auto encoder-decoder model developed by our team to efficiently remove haze.

Our algorithm follows a multi-modal approach, effectively balancing both dark and light haze.

It also incorporates object detection for enhanced situational awareness.

In comparison to other algorithms, our approach is faster at generating de-hazed frames, which greatly aids in rescue operations.

It seamlessly processes input from various sources, like cameras, and delivers real-time output through our user-friendly web interface.

Allows users to upload videos for on-demand de-hazed output.
