RNA-sequencing Based Cancer Detection

RNA sequencing based cancer detection using deep learning algorithms.

Content:

  • The aim of this study is to introduce an innovative technique for cancer classification through the design of a bespoke neural network empowered by hybridized Harris Hawk Whale Optimization.
  • This approach can handle RNA-seq data and facilitate the precise categorization of cancer patients with high accuracy.
  • This research provides a promising solution to the current challenges in cancer diagnosis. Furthermore, we conducted a comparative analysis between the proposed model and other models, considering a range of performance metrics employed by various classifiers to assess its effectiveness.
  • We also aim to highlight the importance of utilizing advanced computational techniques to overcome the complexity of biological systems.
